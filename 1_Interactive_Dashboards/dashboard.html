📋 F-1 visa process — step by step
1
accept admission & pay deposit
accept your HGSE offer on the portal, pay the $250 deposit. this triggers your I-20!
When: by April 15 | Cost: $250
2
receive I-20 from harvard
harvard's international office (HIO) will mail/email your I-20 form. this is your visa sponsor document. DO NOT lose it!
When: 2-4 weeks after acceptance | Keep it SAFE!
3
pay SEVIS fee (I-901)
pay the $350 SEVIS fee online at fmjfee.com. you need your SEVIS ID from your I-20.
Cost: $350 USD
4
fill DS-160 online form
the big visa application form! fill it out carefully at ceac.state.gov. takes 1-2 hours. save often!
Photo: need a 2x2 inch photo (white background)
5
pay visa fee ($185)
pay the MRV visa application fee at ustraveldocs.com. keep the receipt!
Cost: $185 USD (~₹15,400)
6
book visa interview at US Consulate Mumbai
schedule your appointment at ustraveldocs.com. try to book early! mumbai consulate at BKC.
Location: US Consulate General, C-49, G Block, BKC, Mumbai
7
attend visa interview 🎤
dress smart casual, be confident, arrive early! common questions: why harvard? why this program? how will you fund it? plans after graduation?
💡 bring: passport, I-20, DS-160 confirmation, SEVIS receipt, admission letter, financial docs, transcripts, photos
8
receive visa! 🎉
usually 3-5 business days after interview. passport returned with F-1 visa stamp via courier!
Pro tip: you can enter the US up to 30 days before your program start date on the I-20
📑 visa interview documents checklist
❗ valid passport (min 6 months validity beyond program)
❗ I-20 form (from harvard)
❗ DS-160 confirmation page
❗ SEVIS fee receipt (I-901)
❗ visa appointment confirmation
✅ harvard admission letter
❗ financial proof (bank statements, loan sanction letter, scholarship letters)
❗ academic transcripts & degree certificate
❗ passport-size photos (2x2 inch, white background)
❗ sponsor documents (if family is sponsoring)

• Budget: ₹45,000-₹75,000 one-way (book 2+ months early)
• Best airlines: Emirates (via Dubai), Qatar (via Doha), Etihad (via Abu Dhabi)
